The Preto River Falls trail is located within the Chapada dos Veadeiros National Park and is the main and most visited trail. It is approximately 35 km away from the city of Alto Paraíso do Goiás, located in the village of São Jorge. It is certainly one of the destinations worth visiting. The tour has 4 main attractions, which are: 120 meter jump (for photos only), 80 meter jump (Garimão waterfall), Carrossel waterfall and finally Corredeiras.

An unmissable but difficult trail

The route is quite long with an average total distance, round trip, of 13km. There are many slopes and inclines, which makes the route difficult. However, an incredible journey through the savannah with lots of vegetation and wild animals. On the way you can see areas of old crystal mining in the region, which are small deactivated mines.

First stop: 120 meter jumps

The first attraction of the trail is marked by the Mirante dos Saltos, which surprises with a breathtaking landscape and welcomes the blessings of the place. From there you can observe the majestic landscape of the 120 meter jump and the entire intangible valley formed around the great fall. Undeniably one of the most beautiful settings in Chapada dos Veadeiros.  The 120 meter jump can only be seen from above. There are no trails to get to the well.

Second stop: 80 meter jumps or Garimpão waterfall

Walking a little further, about 800 meters, we will reach the 80-meter jump known as the Garimpão waterfall. Surrounded by a huge natural pool and small rapids, this is the first stop to swim, with lots of sun and shade. One of the largest natural pools in the region is formed here.

Third stop: Carousel waterfall

The carousel attraction was recently opened in 2019. The trail was largely made of wooden decks resistant to rain and sun. The path impresses with its grandeur that borders the large canyon of the Rio Preto. A beautiful wooden deck is the postcard of the place, which has a panoramic view of the cerrado with the Carousel waterfall in the background. The fall is smaller than the previous ones, but with a large well for swimming. Visiting is not permitted during the rainy season (November to April), only during the dry season (May to October).

Last stop: rapids

Um caminhada por um terreno mais plano, em torno de 1 km para chegar até o último atrativo: as corredeiras. A incrível jornada termina em uma área de relaxamento e lazer, podendo descansar e desfrutar desse incrível lugar. With a natural hydromassage and several small waterfalls amid virgin nature to end this tour through Chapada dos Veadeiros National Park.

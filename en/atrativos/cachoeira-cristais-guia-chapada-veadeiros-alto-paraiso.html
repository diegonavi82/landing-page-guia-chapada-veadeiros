Crystal Waterfall

Hire a local guide!

Distance from Alto Paraíso: 8 kilometers

Trail Distance (round trip): 2 km

Difficulty Level: medium (all downhill trail)

Attractions:

- Small crystalline wells

- Small waterfalls

- Bride's Veil Waterfall

- Lookout point with panoramic views

Prohibited: R$40

Recommended period for visiting:

Whole year

Recommended for all ages

Cachoeira dos Cristais is located 8 km from Alto Paraíso (5km of asphalt and 3km of dirt) and is made up of several mini waterfalls and wells within its complex. Without a doubt, it is the perfect place to rest and enjoy with the whole family. The waterfall tour begins with Vovó's well (ideal for children and the elderly) which is located 200 meters from the parking lot and ends at the Véu da Noiva waterfall which is approximately 1.5 km from the restaurant, this being the main waterfall.

Great infrastructure

There is a snack bar on site where a variety of snacks are served. On weekends, a buffet lunch is served by the kg. Cold beer is also the place's strong suit. On festive dates it is possible to be surprised by live shows for celebration. Not only does the place live from the waterfall and adventure tourism, nighttime events are also common and generally occur on holidays. There is pool for adults and a bounce house for children as well as a swing. And also slackline. All this facing a spectacular view of the Moinho Valley where macaws fly over daily.

Small wells and waterfalls

The richness of the attraction is made up of small wells of crystal clear water and waterfalls to the sound of the aquifer flow. There are no large falls or large swimming holes at the site. Small hydromassages are formed in some falls, so choose the best place you want to bathe. This is certainly an ideal place to rest and meditate while listening to the sound of the waters and enjoying the day.

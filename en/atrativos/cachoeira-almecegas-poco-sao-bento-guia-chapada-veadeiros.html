The Almécegas and São Bento waterfalls are located just 8 km from Alto Paraíso de Goiás in Chapada dos Veadeiros. This is a very popular itinerary since the distance from the city is very small, as well as the attractions being at the São Bento farm, which has infrastructure with a restaurant, parking, bathrooms, stud farms, signposted trails, maintained dirt road and even craft beer. In addition, the São Bento farm has the largest zipline in Chapada dos Veadeiros, the hawk flight, which needs to be booked in advance if you want to participate. This is the adventure tourism most sought after by travelers.

São Bento Well

The route to the São Bento waterfall or well is the one with the easiest and quickest access. Right at the entrance to the farm, you must park your car before the guardhouse. A small, 300-meter flat trail will lead to a place with a huge well that is very popular with residents for bathing. This waterfall is highly recommended for those with children and the elderly. It has an 8 meter high drop.

Almécegas Waterfall 1

The path to Almécegas 1 waterfall is short, with an approximate length of 1.5 km, but difficult. From the waterfall entrance to the parking lot that marks the beginning of the trail there is 4 km on a well-maintained dirt road with continuous maintenance. A steep climb will mark the beginning of the trail to the top of the hill where you must walk for a few meters until you reach the pool area (which are on top of the waterfall) and the viewpoint in a flat area. It is recommended that you go to the pools after going to the waterfall, as it is a place of relaxation and marks the halfway point on the way back. Therefore, the first stop is the wooden viewpoint with a direct view of the waterfall. After being dazzled by the magic of the waterfall, a huge slope with high steps until you reach the Almécegas 1 waterfall. Without a doubt, it is the most beautiful of the 3. A huge pool of icy waters marks the landscape. The waterfall has numerous small falls, like a water staircase, as a result it causes a meditative sound of flowing waters. Surrounded by rocks and lots of native forest, the Almécegas I waterfall is undoubtedly an important temple in the cerrado.

Almécegas Waterfall 2

The trail to the Almécegas 2 waterfall is very short, just 300 meters, and easy to access, the entire route being flat. Its fall is approximately 10 meters high, with a large well for swimming and also for a small jump of approximately 5 meters. At the top there are several very shallow natural pools. A great option for those traveling with children and wanting to enjoy a calmer moment. Next to the waterfall there are straight and smooth rocks, a good place to sit and rest while enjoying the view.

Voo do Gavião zip line

For those who enjoy adventure tourism in Chapada dos Veadeiros, the São Bento farm offers a zipline 850 meters long and 100 meters high, at an average speed of 55km/h. There are two ways to go down the zip line: sitting or lying down like superman. The activity is monitored by experienced drivers fromecotourism crossingthat use techniques with internationally known procedures and ABNT NBR ISO 21101 safety standards.

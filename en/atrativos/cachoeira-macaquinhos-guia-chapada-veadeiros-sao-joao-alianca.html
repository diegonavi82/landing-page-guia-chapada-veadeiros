Macaquinhos Waterfalls

Hire a local guide!

Distance from Alto Paraíso: 44 km (31 km of which are dirt roads)

Trail Distance (round trip): 6 km

Difficulty Level: difficult (lots of stairs)

Attractions:

- Monkey Bath Waterfall

- Serene Well

- Lunna Waterfall

- Pedra Furada Waterfall

- Banho Pelado Waterfall

- Poço do Jump

- Waterfall of Light

- Cave Waterfall

- Natural pools

- Canyons

Individual entry: R$50

Recommended period for visiting:

The whole year

Not recommended for children up to 6 years old and the elderly

It is recommended to go with a local guide

THEMacaquinhos river waterfall complexis located approximately 44 km from the city of Alto Paraíso de Goiás in Chapada dos Veadeiros. An attraction that has 9 large waterfalls and swimming holes along its trail. The main one is thecave waterfall, a huge drop of approximately 50 meters that is next to a cave. Further below, theEncontro waterfall.The place is the only one that offers a waterfall for nudism, theNaked Bath.A difficult route to access but worth every second. Other attractions on site:Luz waterfall, Jump well, Lunna waterfall, Sereno well, Pedra Furada and Macacos bath.

Difficult access and trail

To get to the Macaquinhos waterfall entrance in Chapada dos Veadeiros, you need to take the BR-020 towards Brasília for 13 km leaving Alto Paraíso. A huge sign will indicate the beginning of the dirt road on the left. From that point it will be another 31 km. The road is not well signposted and there may not be signs at the forks so it is easy to get lost. It takes around 1 hour by car to reach the parking sign for unmarked cars. It is very important that everyone who does not have a 4x4 car stops their vehicle at this sign as the last km has a vertical descent in terrible conditions that is very difficult to climb. The trail lasts approximately 6km round trip and is difficult to access, with a lot of slope and stairs.It is not recommended for the elderly or children under 5 years old.

Cave Waterfall

The main waterfall of the attraction is Cachoeira da Caverna, one of the monuments of Chapada dos Veadeiros. With a drop of approximately 65 meters, it forms a huge pit on the edge of a cave carved into the canyon. With many stone plateaus, this is the main resting point for tourists who can swim and enjoy the beauty of the place while watching birds flying around the large well.

Encounter Waterfall

The last stop on the trail, the meeting waterfall is formed by the monkey and monkey rivers with two vertical drops. It forms a narrow well with a lot of current due to the force of falling into the water. A place of great magic in Chapada dos Veadeiros.

Poço do Jump and Cachoeira da Luz

As the name suggests, the Jump well gets its name because it has a stone platform that serves as a springboard for its well.We do not recommend jumping, as the risk of hitting a rock at the bottom can cause irreversible consequences.So enter the well calmly and admire the surrounding landscape without risk. The waterfall of light, which is just below is also connected to this magnificent place.

Naked Bath

Perhaps the only place to practice naturism in the Chapada dos Veadeiros region. For everyone who feels the call to be more connected to nature through nudism, this is the place. A small well to enjoy and enjoy this moment of connection with mother nature.

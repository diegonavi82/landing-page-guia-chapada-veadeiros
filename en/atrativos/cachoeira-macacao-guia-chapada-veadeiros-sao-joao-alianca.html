Monkey Waterfall

Hire a local guide!

Distance from Alto Paraíso: 47 km (34km of dirt road)

Trail Distance (5km)

Difficulty Level: Difficult

Attractions:

- Muralha Waterfall

- Almécegas Waterfall 1000

- Cachoeira do Parafuso

- Buracão Waterfall

- Natural Pools

- Prainhas

- Canyons

- Lookouts

Prohibited: R$50

Recommended period for visiting:

Dry season from April to October

Mandatory guide

Buy your tour!The Macacão river waterfall complex is without a doubt one of the best routes to visit in Chapada dos Veadeiros. A totally isolated place, which can only be reached with a local guide, which is mandatory at the attraction, and with a 4x4 vehicle. This is one of the last refuges on planet Earth still untouched by man, with wild nature. Wood deer and maned wolves are frequently seen in the area, which prefer to isolate themselves in places without the presence of humans.

How to get there

Access is via the same road that leads to the complex.Macaquinhos waterfalls. There will be a fork to the left after approximately 6km from the start of the dirt road, where a real challenge will begin as this route is dangerous even for 4x4 vehicles, so all attention is necessary. There will be many forks along the way without any type of signage, which is why it is mandatory to go with a local guide. In addition to preserving the safety of the tour, the guide will know exactly the route to get there. There will be river crossings, so it is necessary to avoid the attraction on rainy days as there is a risk of not being able to continue at a certain point on the road.

Monkey Village

Near the beginning of the trail, there will be rustic buildings with stone walls and thatched roofs, where a family lives who takes care of the entrance to the attraction. It is necessary to stop at the entrance and identify yourself, in addition to paying the entrance fee. Beautiful buildings that resemble an indigenous village.

About the trail

The trail is difficult but short, approximately 5km round trip. There are many spots for swimming, with viewpoints, natural pools, small beaches and huge waterfalls: Staircase Waterfall, Cathedral Waterfall and Missing Link Waterfall. As few people venture into this place, the trail is heavily forested and all care is required. At all times the trail goes alongside the river. The most difficult part is on the way down to the Catedral waterfall, which is very vertical and you need to be careful not to slip or twist your foot at this point.

Escadaria Waterfall

After 500 meters when starting the descent, it is now possible to enjoy the first bath of the tour at the Escadaria waterfall. A vertical drop with large stone steps. To get to the waterfall well, you need to go around the waterfall from above and go down the side in a very vertical and dangerous place. If you have difficulty, stay in the natural pools above the Escadaria waterfall.

Prainha, natural pools and Top of the Cathedral

As you continue, a beautiful little beach that resembles a spa will be seen just after the Escadaria waterfall. The place is super welcoming and peaceful, with a beautiful landscape overlooking the canyon that flows into the Escadaria waterfall. Walking a little further in a zigzag along the river, several natural pools with a stone plateau for resting, it is difficult to choose a place to rest in front of so many beautiful landscapes. At the end, the top of the Cathedral waterfall with a deep well for swimming. A spectacular view from the top of the mountain with a viewpoint from the top of the Catedral waterfall to the Monkey River valley. Breathtaking.

Cathedral Waterfall

It is the main waterfall of the attraction with a drop of approximately more than 100 meters that flows into two gigantic pools. And it even forms small waterfalls between one and the other. It's difficult to choose a place to bathe in the face of so much immensity. This is a sacred place on the planet, a great magical portal. It is impossible not to be moved by the grandeur of the Macacão River and the formation of the Catedral waterfall. A break of at least 1 hour is necessary here.

Missing Link Waterfall

Continuing along the Macacão River after the Catedral waterfall, two huge pools until reaching the top of Cachoeira do Elo Perdido. This is the end point of the tour. From there it is possible to see the continuation of the river with more falls that are formed in a large valley of canyons. The place is very dangerous so it is important to be careful at all times. The view from the top of the Elo Perdido waterfall is infinite.

Label Waterfall

Hire a local guide!

Distance from Alto Paraíso: 97 km (26 km of dirt road)

Trail Distance (round trip): 4 km

Difficulty Level: moderate

Attractions:

- Label Waterfall (187 meters)

- Small waterfalls

- Natural Pools

- Canyon

Prohibited:

R$30 with guide

R$50 without guide

Lunch: R$45

Recommended period for visiting:

Whole year

It is recommended to go with a local guide

Located within the municipality of São João da Aliança, Portal da Chapada dos Veadeiros, theLabel waterfallIt is 187 meters high and is considered the largest waterfall in the state of Goiás, as well as being among the six largest in the country. This majestic waterfall has five natural wells/pools and a beautiful wall. Located in the Bellatrix Reserve, a Private Natural Heritage Area (RPPN) 25km from the municipality of São João d’Aliança, the farm where the waterfall is located was transformed into a reserve in 2016 and, in the last two years, the area gained trails, signage, a restaurant and space for camping in order to attract tourists.How to get thereLeaving Alto Paraíso towards Brasília, the distance traveled will be 69km to São João da Aliança. The waterfall has signposts upon arrival in the city. From there, it's a 26km dirt road to the attraction. The route can be done by any type of vehicle, but very low cars may have difficulty with the irregularities of the terrain in some sections. Access to the waterfall requires a 2 km round trip walk. The trail is moderately difficult and, although the last 200 meters are a little more complex, there are no major obstacles for people with children. The route is not recommended for tourists with mobility problems.

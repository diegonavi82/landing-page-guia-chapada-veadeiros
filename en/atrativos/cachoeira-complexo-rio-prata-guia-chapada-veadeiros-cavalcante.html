Mandatory Guide

The Rio da Prata waterfall complex has 8 main attractions and is located in a remote region of Chapada dos Veadeiros. The attractions are:Silver, 3 Marias, Curtain, Hidden, Passer, King of Silver, Queen of Silver and Emerald.One of the most beautiful places in Chapada dos Veadeiros and still little explored by its visitors due to the complexity of getting to the place. The attraction features natural pools, geological formations with canyons, viewpoints, panoramic landscapes with incredible waterfalls with clear, transparent water. Everything the cerrado can offer exists in this place.

Private properties with fee and entry only with an accredited guide

The River Plate waterfall complex is divided into two privately owned areas. The first part covers the first five waterfalls, and if you continue the trail to the last attractions, which are: Escondida, Rainha and Rei do Prata waterfalls, there will be new charges along the way. It is not permitted to enter the site without an accredited tour guide. The River Plate is an inhospitable place, therefore the measure tends to guarantee the safety of visitors, avoiding accidents and losses. Talk to us and hire an accredited guide.

Heavy Trail and Long Distance

To get to the entrance to the Prata waterfall, it is necessary to travel a distance of approximately 65 km from Cavalcante on a dirt road in poor conditions. Leaving Alto Paraíso, the trip tends to last around 3 hours, so it is very tiring. The ideal time to visit the River Plate waterfalls would be two days, so that you can do the entire itinerary calmly, stopping at all the attractions. The entire trail is approximately 14 km round trip and difficult.

First property: Cachoeira Pratinha, Três Marias, Esmeralda, Cortina and Passador

Located just 500 meters from the parking lotPratinha waterfallIt shows all its beauty on a wall with 4 similar falls into a shallow pool of crystal clear waters forming a small pool. Coming out of the Pratinha waterfall is the Mars Valley, which is very similar to the Moon Valley, with circular craters forming natural pools. In this sense will be the next fall of the three Marias. With three streams of water flowing into a natural pool. Very similar to Pratinha, but with thinner falls as if they were going down a natural pipe carved over the years. Soon after, the Esmeralda waterfall, which has emerald green water color. Thirdly, the Cortina waterfall, which falls into a small vertical wall, also forming a natural pool. In fact, all the attractions form a swimming pool. And finally, on the first property, the Passador waterfall, which has a drop like a slide with an inclined wall.

Second property: Cachoeira Escondida, Rainha e Rei do Prata

The hidden waterfall is difficult to access so your stop is just to enjoy the viewpoint with the pool below. The risk is not worth the descent. Just below is the Rei do Prata, the main attraction of the River Plate waterfall complex. Formed over a forest corridor, the canyon forms a true green cauldron, both water and forest surround this precious savannah. Crystal clear water in a well surrounded by vertical walls, great for climbing lovers. The waterfall itself is not the biggest, but the landscape is from another planet. And finally the Rainha waterfall, which has very difficult and complicated access.

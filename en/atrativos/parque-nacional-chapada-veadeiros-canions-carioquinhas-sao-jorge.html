The Canyons trail in the Chapada dos Veadeiros national park is generally the second option among visitors as it is less attractive than the Saltos do Rio Preto trail. Above all, a trail of extreme beauty and ease of an average level combined with two beautiful attractions: the Canyons and the Cariocas waterfall.

A light trail with lots of nature

The route is long but with a medium level of difficulty. The trail has an incredible wealth of varieties of native savanna and with a small difference in level of only 250 meters along the entire path, which makes the trail extremely pleasant.

Canyons

One of the attractions, the canyons, show the force of the waters passing between stone corridors with rock formations that are billions of years old, intact and naturally sculpted. The Canyon has a bathing area in two huge natural pools, formed at the end of the water corridor. A mandatory stop at these two pools is necessary.

Cariocas Waterfall

Leaving the canyons, you need to walk another 1 km to reach the Cariocas waterfall. A steep descent will take you to the main well, bathed by the Cariocas waterfall. Several pools and natural showers are formed by this waterfall, which is quite extensive horizontally. The site is surrounded by natural quarries and native savannah. The waters are dark and belong to the Preto River, with a Coca-Cola hue. Buy your tour!

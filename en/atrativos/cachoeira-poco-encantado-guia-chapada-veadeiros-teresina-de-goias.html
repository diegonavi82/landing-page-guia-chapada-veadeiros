Poço Encantado Waterfall

Hire a local guide!

Distance from Alto Paraíso: 53km

Trail Distance (round trip): 1 km

Difficulty Level: easy

Attractions:

- Enchanted Well Waterfall

- River

- Prainha

- Standup Paddling

Prohibited: R$50

Recommended period for visiting:

Whole Year

Recommended for all ages

The Poço Encantado waterfall in Chapada dos Veadeiros is undoubtedly the best destination for families and elderly people. Easily accessible, it has a huge beach in front of the waterfall where children can play at the water's edge while being watched by lifeguards who guarantee greater safety. There is also a Standup paddle option for those looking for adventure. The farm where Poço Encantado is located has a great infrastructure with a restaurant, bathrooms, a path paved with stones to the bathing place and chalets for rent.

How to Get There? How much does it cost?

Leaving Alto Paraíso it takes around 53 km to the entrance to the waterfall on the right. A huge sign will indicate the direction. A dirt road of approximately 1 km will lead to the parking lot, but first you will need to arrange your individual ticket at the entrance gate. Parking is free.  The trail is easy, just 500 meters, 100% paved with stones and a steel cable bridge.

Great infrastructure with accessibility

A typical food restaurant is located next to the parking lot from where you can see the Poço Encantado waterfall from outside. Architecture with cactus, fountain, well-equipped bathrooms, stone mosaic... everything is top notch including the service. The chalets are all modern, with views of the Poço Encantado waterfall and a small artificial stream with carp and other exotic fish. There is also a swimming pool at the top of the hill facing the Waterfall, but only guests can use this area. The place is best suited for people with mobility difficulties.

Prainha do Cerrado

Certainly one of the strong points of the attraction of the Poço Encantado waterfall is its little beach. A huge space of sand suitable for resting that really resembles a coastal beach. It is possible to rest in the shade formed by trees that surround the sand while enjoying the enormous fall of the Poço Encantado waterfall from a vertical wall. It is an ideal place for children, wheelchair users and seniors.
